1. Break a egg and put the insides in a bowl.

2. Along with egg, put chopped onions, salt, green chilli, pepper

3. stir the above mix well for 5 to 10 mins.

4. take a pan and put some oil and place it in the stove.

5. When you think it is heated pour the above like a omlete.

6. Place two breads and put mayonise on one bread and pudina chutney on other bread.

7. Fold the omlead and the bread wola bread omlete is ready.

8. Have some ketchup.
